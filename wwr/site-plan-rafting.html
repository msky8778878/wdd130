Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help our potential customers to reach us - to be more visible to the public. People want to know more details before trying something new - through this website they are able to check out our services and learn more about our business.

Audience

1.Who are our target customers? - People, both young and old, who love sports, water, adventure and nature. 2.What do they want? Families want to fun together, while youth groups want to have thrilling adventure. 3.How will my audience usually access my site? Most likely using laptop and mobile devices, as those who usually visit us are on their vacations, or are camp leaders organizing trips for their groups.
